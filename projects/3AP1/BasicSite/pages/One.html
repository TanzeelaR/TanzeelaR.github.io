Coleman Bark's Translation

Out beyond ideas

of wrongdoing
and right-doing,

there is a field.

I’ll meet you there.



Translation by Shargh Zadeh

Beyond kufr and Islam
there is a desert plain,

in that middle space
our passions reign.

When the gnostic arrives there
he’ll prostrate himself,

not kufr not Islam nor is there any
space in that domain.
